1Install on Your Phone

No App Store needed. Add it to your home screen like a normal app — takes 30 seconds.

📱 iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open Safari and go to this page.
  2. Tap the Share button — the box with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen."
  4. Tap Add. Done — the app icon appears on your home screen.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome and go to this page.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner.
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen."
  4. Tap Add. Done.
💡 TipOnce installed, open it from your home screen like any other app. It works even with spotty signal.

2Track a Trip with GPS

The app uses your phone's GPS to measure how far you drove — automatically.

  1. Open the app. You'll land on the Track tab.
  2. Tap Instacart, DoorDash, or Other to pick your platform.
  3. Tap "Start Trip."If asked for location permission, tap Allow.
  4. Drive your route. The app tracks in the background and shows a live timer.
  5. When you're done, tap "End Trip."
  6. A popup shows your GPS-estimated miles. Adjust if needed, then tap Save.
💡 TipIf GPS wasn't running, just type in the miles manually on the End Trip screen — no problem.

3Log a Past Trip

Forgot to track? Add it after the fact.

  1. On the Track tab, tap "Log Past Trip" (below the Start Trip button).
  2. Enter the date, platform, and miles.
  3. Add a note if you want, then tap Save.

4See Your Tax Deduction

The app calculates your IRS deduction automatically — $0.70 per mile (2025 standard rate).

  1. Tap the Summary tab at the bottom.
  2. Use the Year and Month dropdowns to pick the period you want.

You'll see:

  • Total miles driven
  • Your total tax deduction in dollars
  • A breakdown by platform
💡 TipSet month to "All Months" to see your full-year total — that's the number you need at tax time.

5View & Delete Trips

  1. Tap the History tab.
  2. Scroll through your trips. Each card shows the date, platform, miles, and deduction.
  3. To delete a trip, tap the trash icon on that card and confirm.

6Export to CSV for Taxes

Download all your trips as a spreadsheet — perfect for your accountant or tax software.

  1. Go to the Summary tab.
  2. Select the year you want.
  3. Tap "Export CSV."
  4. The file downloads to your device. Open it in Excel or Google Sheets.It includes every trip plus a totals row at the bottom.
💡 TipSave this file somewhere safe — it's your official mileage log for taxes.
